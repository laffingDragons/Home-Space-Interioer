About Us

Hanger space interiors moves forward with the vision to create a high-quality interior design for the infrastructures of the world. Over the years, we have been working continuously to deliver best quality decoration in a cost-effective manner. We take pride in creating amazing interiors for offices, corporates, houses, auditoriums and much more. Our team has performed an appreciable task of satisfying clients with their high level of focus and creativity. passionate about creativity and core knowledge on colors, shapes, spaces and textures have been the strengths of our team, hence we are not just interior design company, we are much beyond that. The founders have given their vast expertise to the industry as creative head and were an integral part of building the various brand from scratch. The experience in advertising, brand building, visual designing and user experience for major brands of the globe have made the thought process stronger and know how exactly the project has to be managed and delivered within the said time lines and with the classy finish
